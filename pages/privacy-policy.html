🔒 Privacy Policy

AVUIX ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our gaming platform.

1. Information We Collect

Usage Data

We collect minimal data about your interaction with our platform, including:

  • Game selections and playtime
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • General geographic location (country level)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal identification information (unless voluntarily provided)
  • Detailed location data
  • Behavioral tracking across the web
  • User content or communications

2. How We Use Your Information

Any collected data is used solely to:

  • Improve platform performance and stability
  • Provide technical support
  • Prevent abuse and security threats
  • Generate anonymized analytics

3. Data Retention

We retain usage data for a maximum of 90 days. After this period, data is automatically purged from our systems. You can request deletion at any time.

4. Cookies & Tracking

AVUIX uses minimal cookies for essential functionality only:

  • Session management
  • User preferences
  • Security tokens

We do NOT use:

  • Third-party advertising trackers
  • Behavioral targeting cookies
  • Analytics services that share data

5. External Links & Third Parties

Our platform links to external proxies and services. We are NOT responsible for their privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their individual privacy policies before use.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • DDoS protection
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure data storage

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Port your data to another service

8. Children's Privacy

AVUIX does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data immediately. We encourage parents to monitor their children's online activities.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be announced on our platform. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions? Your privacy is paramount. If you have concerns about how we handle your data, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're committed to transparency and accountability.
